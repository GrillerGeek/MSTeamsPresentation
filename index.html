What is a Koan?

koan

[ˈkōän]

NOUN
a paradoxical anecdote or riddle, used in Zen Buddhism to demonstrate the inadequacy of logical reasoning and to provoke enlightenment.

https://www.anaconda.com/

Install VS Code


https://code.visualstudio.com/
